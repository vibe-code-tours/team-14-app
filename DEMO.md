### HOOK (0:05 — 0:35) — 30 seconds

**[Speaker 1]**

> Myanmar migrant workers take jobs in Thailand without knowing if a factory is safe
> or if an agency will exploit them. They have no way to check before they commit.
> This puts thousands of workers at risk every year.

---

### SOLUTION (0:35 — 1:05) — 30 seconds

**[Speaker 2]**

> We built WorkerVoice. It is a platform where workers can anonymously review
> factories and recruitment agencies. Before accepting a job, a worker can search
> for a company, read real reviews from other workers, and make a safer decision.
> Anonymous reviews protect the worker's identity while giving the community
> the information it needs.

---

### LIVE DEMO (1:05 — 4:30) — 3 minutes 25 seconds

**[Speaker 3 — narrate while performing clicks on the live URL]**

> Let me show you how it works.

#### Step 1 — Landing page (1:05 — 1:25)

**Click:** Open `[YOUR LIVE URL]` in a clean browser (incognito).

**Say:**
> This is the WorkerVoice homepage. You can see the platform is in English and
> Myanmar. Let me search for a factory.

**Viewer sees:** Hero section with search bar, platform stats, and language toggle.

#### Step 2 — Factory search (1:25 — 1:50)

**Click:** Type a factory name in the search bar, or click "Browse Factories."

**Say:**
> I am looking for a specific factory. Let me browse the full list. You can filter
> by region and type. Here are the factories that have been added to the platform.

**Viewer sees:** Factory listing page with filters and factory cards.

#### Step 3 — Factory detail + reviews (1:50 — 2:30)

**Click:** Click on a factory card to open the detail page.

**Say:**
> This is the factory detail page. I can see the factory name, location, and
> company information. Below that, I can see anonymous reviews from workers who
> have actually worked there. Each review has a star rating and written feedback.
> Let me scroll through these reviews.

**Viewer sees:** Factory detail page with star ratings and review cards.

#### Step 4 — Submit a review (2:30 — 3:15)

**Click:** Click "Write a Review" button. Sign in if needed (or show the form).

**Say:**
> To leave a review, you need an account. Registration is quick and your real
> name is never shown publicly. Now I can rate this factory and write about my
> experience. My identity stays anonymous. Let me submit this review.

**Viewer sees:** Review form with star rating, text input, and submit button.

#### Step 5 — The payoff (3:15 — 3:45)

**Click:** Show the review appearing on the factory page after submission.

**Say:**
> My review is now live on the factory page. Other workers can see this and
> make better decisions. One review can protect dozens of workers from a bad
> situation. That is the core of WorkerVoice.

**Viewer sees:** The newly submitted review appears in the review list.

#### Step 6 — Agency listing (3:45 — 4:10)

**Click:** Navigate to the Agencies page.

**Say:**
> WorkerVoice also covers recruitment agencies. Workers can check if an agency
> has good reviews before signing a contract. This is just as important as
> checking the factory itself.

**Viewer sees:** Agency listing page.

#### Step 7 — Admin portal preview (4:10 — 4:30)

**Click:** Navigate to the admin dashboard (log in as admin).

**Say:**
> On the admin side, we have a full management portal. Admins can approve or
> reject factories, moderate reviews, and manage users. This keeps the platform
> trustworthy.

**Viewer sees:** Admin dashboard with stats cards and management menus.

---

### TECH HIGHLIGHT (4:30 — 5:15) — 45 seconds

**[Speaker 4]**

> The hardest part of this project was building the full admin portal with
> role-based access control in a short time. We have four user roles with
> different permissions, and every admin action is protected.
>
> We used Claude Code to scaffold the admin routes, generate the Prisma schema
> models, and build the API endpoints. What would have taken days was completed
> in hours. The AI helped us write consistent, type-safe code across the entire
> admin feature set.

---

### WHAT'S NEXT (5:15 — 5:45) — 30 seconds

**[Speaker 5]**

> For the real user, the next step is Telegram bot integration. Many Myanmar
> workers use Telegram as their main app. A bot would let workers search for
> factory reviews directly from Telegram without downloading anything new.
> We would also add AI-powered review moderation to automatically detect
> and filter fake or harmful reviews.

---

### OUTRO (5:45 — 6:00) — 15 seconds

**[All team members together]**

> That is WorkerVoice. Built by Team 14. Thanks for watching.
